Lasagna Recipe

This easy Lasagna Recipe is meaty, cheesy, and flavorful. Perfect for a weeknight dinner or feeding a crowd, readers agree it's the best!

Ingredients

  1. 1/2 pound dry lasagna noodles (requires 9 lasagna noodles - unbroken)
  2. 2 jars meat sauce
  3. 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  4. 1 1/2 pounds (24 ounces) mozzarella cheese, grated or sliced
  5. 1/4 pound (4 ounces) freshly grated Parmesan cheese
